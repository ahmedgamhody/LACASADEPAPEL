la casa de papel

La Casa de Papel, also known as House of Cards or House of Money, and better known as The Professor as promoted by Netflix Middle East and North Africa, is a Spanish robbery series produced by Alex Pena for the Antena 3 network. It is a limited series that aired for five seasons and was shown for the first time. On May 2, 2017 and for the last time on December 3. 2021. Number of seasons: 5 Number of episodes: 41
